Column Reference

These are the required and optional columns:

Personal Information

id Unique student ID (number)
name Student's full name
email Student email address
address City or home address
grade Grade level (9, 10, 11, 12)

Family Information

fo Father's occupation
fq Father's education level
mo Mother's occupation
mq Mother's education level
tt Travel time to school (hours)

Academic Performance

ias Internal Assessment Score (0-100)
twp Previous grade score
tnp Two grades ago score
arr Exam failure status (Y/N)

Attendance & Engagement

attendance Attendance percentage (0-100)
teacher_rating Teacher rating (1-5 scale)
nf Number of close friends
fee_late Fee payment status

Health & Support

previous_visits Counseling visits count
summary_self_check_in Mental health summary
teacher_notes Teacher notes/observations

Tips for Successful Upload

  • Ensure all required columns are present
  • Use consistent data formats (e.g., grades as numbers)
  • Remove any empty rows or columns
  • Check for duplicate student IDs
  • Attendance should be 0-100, not 0-1
